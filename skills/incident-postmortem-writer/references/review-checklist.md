# Review Checklist

## Before Circulation

Confirm these items before sharing the draft:

- incident ID, service, severity, and time window are correct
- customer impact matches the incident record
- timeline events are in the right order
- root cause wording avoids blame and unsupported claims
- action items are concrete and have owners
- placeholder text is gone

## Sensitive Detail Check

Scrub or generalize as needed:

- customer names
- security detail
- internal-only architecture references
- privileged operator notes

## Approval Path

For broad internal or external circulation, keep:

- at least one human review
- an explicit approval point when the incident is high severity or customer-visible
